KMS Evolution

Since its inception more than four decades ago, KMS has functioned as a solid partnership bound by a shared set of principles and the desire to bring about long-lasting, constructive change. Founded in 1981 in Ahmedabad, KMS started off by providing corporate, legal, tax, and advisory services. Within just five years of business, the entity grew to offer legal advice, project and implementation management, fundraising, initial public offerings, and structuring. The following few decades turned out to be a time of expansion, innovation, collaborations, and partnerships. Turnaround and revival advisory, microfinance, venture funds, AIF, investment & structuring advisory, business strategic advisory, SEZ & Industrial Park, corporate governance, cross-border business expansion, succession planning & management, family office, technology business initiative, strategic investment, and entrepreneur mentoring & support are just a few of the advisory services the entity has diversified to offer.

KMS has more than 100 clients across regions and sectors, including technology, manufacturing, infrastructure, and finance. KMS operates under the highest ethical and professional standards as a value-driven company. In addition to the rapid expansion, KMS is combining innovative tactics and game-changing technology to help businesses innovate, achieve long-term performance improvements, and build a solid organizational framework that will serve them far into the future.

The Team Behind KMS

Mr. Vinod Modha, a chartered accountant by profession with a keen interest in finance and taxation, brings a refined and pragmatic approach and imparts his knowledge and expertise in his work in corporate & financial advisory & consultancy. With rich and extensive experience working with corporates and promoters, he has served on the board of several corporates before taking on the role of strategic advisor for promoters of mid- and large-scale corporates.

His handpicked assembly of celebrated professionals possess unparalleled expertise in advisory, consulting, handholding, and corporate strategy. An unwavering passion for excellence and a tireless commitment to delivering exemplary services to clients is what unites them in this journey of providing businesses with unparalleled success. Under the expert guidance of the KMS team, many organizations have had successful endeavors in their business journey. This team has been instrumental in providing strategic advice to some of the multinational corporations for their business expansion. Their expertise and experience have been crucial in providing wealth management and planning, succession planning, family office, and other advisory services to Indian Promoter Families.
